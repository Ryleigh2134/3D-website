Gunto

The Gunto is one of my favourite swords of all time due to my love for japanese history and the blend of both japanese and western swords to make a recognizable japanese sword that still has a noticeable western influence and shows japanese westernisation and future dominance of its area.


Specifications

  • Slightly curved blade.
  • Gold accents around handgaurd and pommel.
  • A rough red material on the hilt to represent fabric.
  • Different materials on the blade to look more realistic.
