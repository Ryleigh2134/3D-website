About me

I have chosen to make my website due to my love of coding, 3d design and sharing my work. More specifically, I have chosen to make a range of swords due to my personal love for the look, the method of making a sword and the long history of their use throughout the entire world. I hope that this site will reach like-minded people who will enjoy learning a bit about my favorite swords and my decision to make each one. I hope to continue making and improving my designs for my followers so I can one day work in game design and to continue showing my designs to my followers. I hope that my progress can also inspire more people to do what they like whether it be 3d design or something completely different. These are also just my personal versions of these swords and should be further looked into as I am not an expert just someone interested in swords.
