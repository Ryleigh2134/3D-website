Jian

I have made a jian to my love for the simplistic straight design and its long time use throughout the Chinese area and my interest in Chinese history and tactics around their sword making and fighting.


Specifications

  • Long straight blade.
  • Gold accents around handgaurd and pommel.
  • A rough red material on the hilt to represent fabric.
  • Custom matelic silver for blade.
