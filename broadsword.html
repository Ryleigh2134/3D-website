Broadsword

I have chosen to make a broadsword due its classic look and its use throughout many media I like, like video games and films. The broadsword is often a default sword for European middle ages alongside swords like the claymore and longsword.


Specifications

  • Straight wide blade
  • Gold accents around handgaurd and pommel.
  • Rough brow handle.
  • Custom matelic silver for blade.
